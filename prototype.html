One-way backup
Source changes copy to destination. Deletions on source are not replicated — files remain on destination.
One-way mirror
Destination becomes an exact mirror of source. Deletions on source are replicated to destination.
Two-way
Changes on either side propagate to the other. Conflicts are detected and queued for resolution.
